What We Love to Do as an Orphan Community


  1. Organize weekly group activities to foster teamwork and bonding.
  2. Engage in creative arts such as painting, drawing, and crafting.
  3. Participate in educational workshops to enhance learning and life skills.
  4. Enjoy sports and outdoor games to stay active and healthy.
  5. Host storytelling sessions to inspire and share personal experiences.
  6. Collaborate in community service projects to give back and build empathy.
  7. Celebrate cultural festivals and events to connect with traditions.
  8. Attend motivational talks and mentoring sessions for guidance and encouragement.
  9. Form study groups to support academic progress and peer learning.
  10. Organize charity drives to collect and distribute essentials for the less fortunate.
  11. Engage in gardening and environmental activities to promote sustainability.
  12. Create a supportive and loving environment for personal and community growth.





Our Community Aspirations and Activities


  1. Participating in weekly storytelling sessions to share life lessons and experiences.
  2. Creating art projects like painting murals or crafting to express creativity.
  3. Attending educational workshops to enhance knowledge and skills.
  4. Organizing nature walks to appreciate and learn about the environment.
  5. Writing letters of gratitude to our supporters and sponsors.
  6. Hosting cultural celebration days to learn about different traditions and customs.
  7. Practicing teamwork through group sports like soccer, volleyball, or badminton.
  8. Exploring books during community reading hours to foster a love of learning.
  9. Preparing meals together to bond and develop practical life skills.
  10. Celebrating personal achievements and milestones to encourage one another.
  11. Cleaning and beautifying shared spaces to build a sense of responsibility and pride.
  12. Organizing peer mentorship programs where older children guide the younger ones.
